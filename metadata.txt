name=Ischia Seismic Scenario

description=Procedure to create an intensity seismic scenario at a local scale of the island of Ischia
version=1.1
author=Salvatore D'Amico - Istituto Nazionale di Geofisica e Vulcanologia
email=salvatore.damico@ingv.it

about=<p>This plugin allows calculating seismic scenarios expressed in terms of macroseismic intensity according to deterministic or probabilistic models of attenuation.<br>The input parameters required to simulate an earthquake, are:<br>- epicentral location, expressed in degrees (xx.yyy);<br>- epicentral intensity (I<sub>0</sub>) or magnitude.<br>In the latter case, the magnitude (Md or MW) is converted into intensity by the relationships obtained by Selva et al. (2021)*.<br><br>Selection of the attenuation models:<br>- deterministic: scenarios for epicentral intensity 6 &le; I<sub>0</sub> &le; 11 (MCS);<br>- probabilistic: scenarios for epicentral intensity 8 &le; I<sub>0</sub> &le; 11 (MCS).<br><br>Results are plotted on map with a grid with node distances of 0.5 km. For the probabilistic scenario, the mode of the distribution is taken as the expected intensity at that site.<br><br>This plugin is a product (supplementary material) related to the paper:<br>Azzaro R, D'Amico S, Rotondi R, Varini E (2023) The attenuation of macroseismic intensity in the volcanic island of Ischia (Gulf of Naples, Italy): comparison between deterministic and probabilistic models and application to seismic scenarios. Bull Earth Eng, submitted.<br><br>*see Table 2 in: Selva J, Azzaro R, Taroni M, Tramelli A, Alessio G, Castellano M, Ciuccarelli C, Cubellis E, Lo Bascio D, Porfido S, Ricciolino P, Rovida A (2021) The seismicity of Ischia Island, Italy: an integrated earthquake catalogue from 8th century BC to 2019 and its statistical properties. Frontiers, Earth Sci 9, 629736.</p>

tracker=https://github.com/saldam33/ischia_seismic_scenario/issues
